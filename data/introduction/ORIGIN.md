# Introduction presentation provenance

`introduction.pin`, `bg.jpg`, `bowls.jpg`, and `linus.jpg` come from the
official Pinpoint 0.1.8 source release, distributed under Pinpoint's
LGPL-2.1-or-later licence.

The historical presentation referred to `bunny.ogv`, but that file was not
included in the 0.1.8 source release. This self-contained edition replaces the
reference with `bunny.webm`, a short excerpt of *Big Buck Bunny* encoded as
VP9/Opus. The source film is Copyright © 2008 Blender Foundation and available
under the Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 licence:

- Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Big_Buck_Bunny_medium.ogv
- Licence: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/
- Attribution: Copyright © 2008 Blender Foundation — https://www.bigbuckbunny.org/

The excerpt begins approximately 25 seconds into the film and is limited to
the portion needed by the demonstration slide.
